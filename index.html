WHY UI DESIGN MATTERS

ABOUT

Behind every modern technology, is UI design - the component, when applied smartly, makes technology more accessible, engaging, and intuitive. Before UI design, computers required fluency with program or machines languages. But now, the playing field is levelled, and aims to be usable to everyone!

User interface (UI) is a space where humans interact with technology or digital products. You might not know it, but you need UI design! Whether you are a student with an upcoming presentation, a young professional seeking their next opportunity, or a business looking to attract the best talent - UI design applies to you.

UI design is your first impression.

User interfaces differ depending on your message, product, and audience.

But they all share the same goal: to make interactions as streamlined and enjoyable as possible.

The most common types of UI include:

Graphical user interfaces (GUIs) →

Image or icon based systems are the most common, and found on many devices. Right now you are interacting with a GUI on your computer screen. User interact with GUIs via a stylus or a mouse. This is also the focus of our webpage!

Gesture-based interfaces →

Augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) are becoming more popular, and therefore, so is gesture-based interfaces. This form translates a user’s motions in a 3D space into commands.

Voice-controlled interfaces (VUIs) →

This relies on he user’s voice to navigate menus and actions. Think about smart assistants like Siri, Alexa, and Google Home - these are all well-known examples of VUIs.
